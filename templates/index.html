FAQ

How do I book an order with CustomFurnish.com?

Ans:You can book an order with us online by clicking on the BOOK NOW buttoon and furnishing all the mandatory information & files.

Is the booking amount refundable?

Ans:If you are opting for the Design only package, then the amount you pay is the design fee and is not refundable. However if you select Design & Execute package, the amount you pay at the time of booking will be absorbed in the total project cost.

Can I switch the package I select at a later point?

Ans:You are free to switch from one package to the other at any time. If you decide to switch from Design Only to Design and Execute, your booking amount will be absorbed in the total project cost. However, if you sign up for Design and Execute package and later decide to avail only design services, we'll not be providing you the Design, BOM & Engineering drawings.
